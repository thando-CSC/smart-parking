What is Smart Parking?

Smart Parking combines IoT sensors, real-time data, mobile apps, and sometimes AI to help drivers find, reserve, and pay for parking quickly — reducing traffic, fuel use, and frustration in busy urban areas like Pretoria.

Key Benefits

  • Live availability right on your phone
  • Less circling = less congestion & emissions
  • Reserve spots & pay cashless
  • LED guidance or app navigation in garages
  • Higher revenue for parking operators
  • Excellent user experience via apps

How Smart Parking Works

Sensors detect if a spot is free → data uploads to the cloud → drivers see real-time info on apps, websites, or signs.
